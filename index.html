About Me

Nicholas Cheong has a Bachelor in Business Management from Singapore Management University (SMU) and a Master of Science in Communication and Media Technologies in 2010 from the Rochester Institute of Technology (RIT).

Nicholas has been working in banking and finance for the last six years. His professional experience spans 14 years in sales, business development, retail, education, the military, and politics.

A former grassroots adviser to the Deputy Prime Minister of Singapore, Nicholas represents Singapore informally in Colorado. He is effectively bilingual in English and Mandarin Chinese and is well-known in the Asian-American and international business communities in Denver. An active member of the community, he has served in various roles in many nonprofit organizations.

Nicholas enjoys reading about history and politics. He is a great cook -- he can cook seven different types of curry! Nicholas is also an expert on feng shui and speaks extensively on the subject.
